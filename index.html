Our Vision

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I sign up for the website? +
To sign up, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details (name, email, password), and submit. A confirmation email will be sent to activate your account.
2. What should I do if I forget my password? +
Simply click on the "Forgot Password" link, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions to reset your password.
3. How can I update my profile details? +
After logging in, go to your profile section and click "Edit Profile" to update your details like name, email, and password.
4. Can I delete my account? +
Yes, you can delete your account by going to the "Account Settings" section and selecting "Delete Account." Please note, this action is irreversible.
1. How can I start a chat with other college students? +
After logging in, navigate to the "Chat" section. You can search for other students by name or course and start a conversation.
2. Can I send images or files through the chat? +
Yes, the chat system allows you to share images, notes, and other files with other students.
3. Can I search for past conversations? +
Yes, you can search your chat history by keywords or specific dates.
4. Is the chat system real-time? +
Yes, the chat system supports real-time messaging, so you can receive messages instantly as they are sent.
1. How does the PYQ solution system work? +
You can search for previous year questions (PYQ) in the relevant subject, and our system will provide solutions using integrated AI tools like ChatGPT or Gemini.
2. Can I upload my own solutions to the PYQ section? +
Yes, you can contribute by uploading your solutions. Admin-approved solutions will be featured on the platform for others.
3. Will my PYQ solutions be available to everyone? +
Yes, once your solutions are approved by the admin, they will be available for all users to view and download.
4. Can I edit my PYQ solutions after uploading? +
You can edit your solutions, but they will require admin approval before being updated on the platform.
1. Who can upload notes to the platform? +
Both students and admins can upload notes. Admin-approved notes will be featured on the platform.
2. Can I download notes uploaded by others? +
Yes, you can download any notes that are uploaded and approved by the admin.
3. How do I search for specific notes? +
You can use the search bar in the "Notes" section to find notes by subject, course, or keyword.
4. Can I rate the notes uploaded by others? +
Yes, you can rate the notes to help others decide if they are helpful or not.
